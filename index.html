About

I started building automations when I noticed how much time teams lose to repetitive manual tasks. That led me to itGenius, where I now build AI-powered document processing pipelines, custom Intercom integrations, and multi-system automations across the Google ecosystem. I also take on freelance projects, most recently building lead-to-accounting pipelines for a Hawaii-based startup.

My work sits at the intersection of automation platforms (Zapier, Make, n8n), AI/LLMs (Gemini, OpenAI, Claude), and custom code (Node.js, PHP, Python). I connect platforms like Intercom, WHMCS, Asana, and Slack through APIs, webhooks, and serverless services on GCP — usually at $0/month to operate.

When I’m not wiring systems together, I’m exploring agentic workflows and building tools with Claude Code, Cursor, and the latest LLM APIs.
